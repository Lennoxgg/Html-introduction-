COMMUNICATION SKILLS

Communication skills are the capacities to exchange information, ideas, and emotions clearly
and effectively through various methods, including verbal, nonverbal, and written forms.

PROBLEM SOLVING SKILLS

Problem-solving skills involve the capacity to recognize and define issues. They include analyzing
the problem, generating and evaluating potential solutions, and implementing the most effective
one. These skills are crucial for effectively addressing challenges and achieving desired outcomes.

ACTIVE LISTENING SKILLS

Active listening is the process of fully concentrating on, understanding, and responding to
a speaker's message. It involves giving full attention,providing feedback, and avoiding interruptions.
This skill helps ensure clear communication and fosters better understanding and relationships.
